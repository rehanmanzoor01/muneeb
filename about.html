About Nundresh Cold Store

Our story, our values, and the people behind Kashmir's trusted cold chain

Our Story

17 Years of Cold Chain Excellence

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Kashmir's apple belt, Nundresh Cold Store began as a focused facility serving local orchardists in Nundresh village. Over nearly two decades, we have grown into one of the valley's most trusted industrial cold storage providers.

Our growth has always followed the needs of our growers. As apple cultivation expanded and markets demanded longer shelf lives, we invested in controlled atmosphere technology, automated monitoring, and expanded capacity — ensuring Kashmir's prized fruit reaches domestic and international markets in perfect condition.

Today, we serve over 500 growers and fruit merchants across Sopore, Shopian, Pulwama, and beyond, with a combined storage capacity exceeding 5,000 metric tonnes.

Mr. Ghulam Nabi Nundresh
Founder & Managing Director

A lifelong resident of Kashmir and a passionate advocate for the valley's apple industry, Mr. Ghulam Nabi Nundresh founded this facility with a vision to empower local growers with the same cold chain infrastructure available in global markets. With over 30 years in the fruit industry, he personally oversees facility operations.

His deep relationships with growers across Sopore, Shopian, and Pulwama — combined with a keen understanding of modern cold chain logistics — have made Nundresh Cold Store synonymous with trust throughout the Kashmir valley.
